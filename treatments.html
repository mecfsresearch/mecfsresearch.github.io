Treatments

The following are trialed, proposed, hypothetical, speculative, or imaginary treatments. These are not meant as treatments for patients to try but ideas for clinical trials and trial updates.

Proposed or Speculative Repurposed Drugs

  • For Orthostatic Hypotension and/or Exercise Intolerance
    • droxidopa (Northera)
    • midodrine
    • fludrocortisone
    • pyridostigmine (Mestinon)
  • For Cerebral Blood Flow
    • sildenafil (Viagra)
      • hypothesis
      • clinical trial completed
      • may improve neurologic and endothelial function and inflammation after brain injury
      • neurological adverse events
      • X discussion
  • low dose naltrexone (LDN) â€“ possible immune modulator, has other effects
  • low dose aripiprazole (Abilify)
  • Immunomodulators/Monoclonal Antibodies
    • rituximab (successful Phase II trial, failed Phase III trial)
    • cyclophosphamide (Fluge & Mella)
    • daratumumab (Fluge & Mella)
    • subcutaneous ofatumumab (Kesimpta) (speculative)
    • next-generation CD20-targeted B cell-depleting antibodies, e.g. obinutuzumab, ocrelizumab and ofatumumab)
      (these deplete both blood and tissue B cells more efficiently than rituximab)
      (proposed by W Robinson et al for autoimmune diseases)
  • rapamycin (Rapamune/Sirolimus) (D Peterson) (clinical trial ongoing)
  • JAK inhibitors, e.g. tofacitinib (Xeljanz) and baricitinib (Olumiant)
  • abatacept or drugs that inhibit antigen presentation
  • checkpoint inhibitors to clear peristent pathogens
    (might cause autoimmunity or rheumatic disease, highly inadvisable outside official clinical trial)
  • IVIG
  • leflunomide or teriflunomide (anti-rheumatic/immune suppressant, EBV inhibitor)
  • Antivirals (new trials should be a minimum 9 months, preferably 12 months)
    • valacyclovir (Valtrex)
    • famciclovir (Famvir)
    • valganciclovir (Valcyte)
    • cidofovir (Vistide)
    • brincidofovir (Tembexa / CMX001)
    • artesunate
    • tenofovir disoproxil fumarate (speculative)
    • tenofovir alafenamide (speculative)

Proposed or Speculative Combination Protocols

  • LDN + pyridostigmine (proposed by D Systrom, clinical trial ongoing)
  • rituximab + anti-EBV drug (proposed by N Klimas & T Craddock, no known plans for trial)
  • rituximab + IVIG (proposed by A Ahmed for generalized autoimmune disease)
  • EBV reactivation agent (e.g. valproic acid) + anti-EBV drug
    (highly speculative, could instigate autoimmunity)
  • bone marrow transplant (highly speculative, dangerous)
